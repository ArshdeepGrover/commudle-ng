Developer Communities by Students & Non-Profits

Commudle is an engine to create a global identity for your Community

Commudle is free to use for independent student led or non-profit tech communities. All features unlocked.

Who Can Avail This Plan

Tech communities led by students or by non-profits which share knowledge for free through any means including events, channels, emails and beyond are eligible.

  1. Unlimited members & organizer team accounts.
  2. Your activites should be completely free for people. However there might be approval based selection to join.
  3. Complete tech and product support is available.

We encourage you to have clear community guidelines for your members to foster learning and help build a strong network.

Join thousands of developers, designers and their communities. Start your own community now!

What is the application process?

  1. Once you have filled the form with appropriate details, our team will reachout in three working day to you from the communication details you have filled in the form.
  2. After we are convinced, we will ask you to share the basic details including the community's logo, name, email and description to create a community.
  3. You will receive a confirmation email with further instructions to start making changes and organizing engagements within your community.

Finally, you & teammates who initiated the conversation will own the community account, Commudle will only act as a platform. However, we are available enthusiastically to help in any ways possible to scale your community by using the platform better.
