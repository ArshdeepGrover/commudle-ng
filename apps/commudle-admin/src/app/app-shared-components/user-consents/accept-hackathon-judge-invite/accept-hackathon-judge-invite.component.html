We are excited to have you join the hackathon as a Judge / Mentor!

As a part of the hackathon, the following information will be shared between you and the organizers and administrators:

  • Your profile will be publicly visible on the hackathon’s page
  • They will have access to your email address
  • You will also by default become a member of the default channel set by the community’s organizers
  • They will send you communication mails and notifications for different events, hackathons, surveys/forms, newsletters and other community activities.
  • They will have access to all the forms, chats, activity in both public and private channels, polls, forums and your other activities for entities within the community.
  • You will automatically become a member of the parent community or organization (if present) of the hackathon and above mentioned member insights will be shared with them
  • They have the right to permit or revoke your membership of the community at any point of time at their discretion.
